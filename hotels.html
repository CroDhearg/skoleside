The Kings Head inn

The King's Head Inn is a small, centuries-old pub in Llangennith, Wales, from the 1600s. It's popular with locals and surfers visiting the nearby beaches. They do nice traditional British food prepared with fresh, local ingredients, and there's a great selection of drinks, including local ales. It also features outdoor seating with nice views, thus making it a perfect place to relax, enjoy good food, and get a feel for some Welsh history and hospitality.
