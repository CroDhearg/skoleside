St Fagans National Museum of History

St. Fagans National Museum of History is a very cool outdoor museum in Cardiff, located in Wales. It's like going back in time to explore old buildings from different parts of Wales. Old farms to a traditional Welsh schools; you get to see how people lived, worked, and played in the past. It has beautiful gardens and other fun things to do. The place probably is the best place to learn about the history of Wales in a fun way.

Cardiff Castle

Cardiff Castle is a majestic and ancient castle set right in the midst of Cardiff in Wales. Its ancient stone walls, towers, and chambers are a combination of both history and beauty. You can remain busy exploring the chambers of the castle, living the life of medieval art, and the incredible view of the city by climbing the towers. The castle grounds are perfect for a stroll, and there is plenty to learn about its history from Roman times to Victorian days. A nice place to visit if you want to feel like you are living in a fairy tale for a day!
